Guest Lectures
Banking Exploration with Matt Anderson

FAI hosted its 3rd guest lecture with Matt Anderson, a Senior Vice President at Wells Fargo Private Bank, at Reedy High School. Our members had the opportunity to learn about the various career pathways open within the banking industry, how to break through in the competitive banking industry, and what the overall banking industry was like.

So You Want to be The Boss? with Winston Black

It is our pleasure to introduce Mr. Winston Black, Founder and Former CEO of SWK Holdings! Mr. Black is a senior life science executive with broad finance, operational and entrepreneurial experience. He most recently served as CEO and Chairman of SWK Holdings Corporation, a Nasdaq-listed, life science specialty finance company that he co-founded in 2012. Prior to that time, Mr. Black was a partner at PBS Capital Management, LLC, an investment management business specializing in pharmaceutical royalties and healthcare equities that he co-founded in 2009. Prior to that, Mr. Black was a Senior Portfolio Analyst at Highland Capital Management, COO/Analyst and Chief Compliance Officer at Mallette Capital Management, and Vice President of Corporate Development for ATX Communications. Mr. Black received MBAs with distinction from both Columbia Business School and London Business School and received a BA in Economics from Duke University, where he graduated Cum Laude.

Exploring Finance Careers with Blake Siewert

In our first guest lecture of the year, we were honored to host Mr. Blake Siewert, the Managing Director at CIBC. Mr. Siewert has extensive experience in finance, including over six years at CIBC and nearly 15 years at J.P. Morgan. During the session, he shared insights on career development in finance, navigating industry challenges, and the importance of networking and continuous learning. The lecture provided valuable knowledge for anyone aspiring to build a career in finance.
